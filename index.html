Tulkarm

Tulkarm has a long history, with evidence of settlement going back to ancient times. The city has been influenced by various civilizations, including the Canaanites, Romans, Byzantines, and Ottomans. During the Ottoman Empire, Tulkarm grew as a market town and became an important administrative center.
